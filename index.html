FAQs

View All
What is Start Up Ayush Portal?
Ayush Portal has been developed by the Ministry for digitalization of various processes involved in all Central Sector Schemes of the Ministry. All major steps will be completed through this portal from receipt of proposals to release of grant/ funding.
What is the Central Sector Scheme?
The Central Sector Schemes are government schemes that are designed, planned and completely funded by the central government.
Which schemes are available under the Central Sector Scheme on Ayush NGO Portal?
The schemes available under Central Sector Scheme are as follows:
1. Central sector scheme for upgradation to centres of excellence (COE)
2. Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information, Education and Communication (IEC) in Ayush
3. Central Sector Scheme for Continuing Medical Education (CME)
4. Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Ayush Intervention in Public Health Initiatives (PHI)
5. Central Sector Scheme for Extra Mural Research (EMR)
6. Champion Services Sector Scheme:
A) Central Sector Scheme for Establishment of Ayush Super Specialty Hospitals
B) Central Sector Scheme for Skill Development in Ayush Sector under Champion Services Sector Scheme for Medical Value Travel
7. Central sector scheme for promotion of International Co-operation (IC) in Ayush
8. Central Sector Scheme on Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants
How to apply for a grant/funding through Ayush NGO Portal?
Interested NGOs are required to submit their details through the respective application form of the scheme and submit their proposal through this portal for funding from the related Central Sector Scheme of the Ministry.
What is the prerequisite for NGOs to apply for grants on this Portal?
NGOs registered on NGO Darpan portal can only apply.
Is this portal integrated with other Portals?
Yes, Ayush NGO Portal is integrated with the NGO Darpan Portal of the Niti Aayog & the Public Financial Management System (PFMS).
